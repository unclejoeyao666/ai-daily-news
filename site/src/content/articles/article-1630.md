ChatGPT is struggling to maintain its explosive growth as users uninstall the app or switch to rival chatbots. Sensor Tower data shows ChatGPT's April uninstall rate surged 132 percent year over year, following a 413 percent spike the prior month after OpenAI's Pentagon deal in February. While still growing, ChatGPT's monthly active user growth slowed from 168 percent in January to 78 percent in April. Notably, Claude's downloads grew 11x during the same period, significantly outpacing ChatGPT's 14 percent growth. CFO Sarah Friar has reportedly expressed concerns about IPO plans, with internal targets for new users and revenue recently missed.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

ChatGPT 的增长神话正在被 Claude 的快速崛起打破。卸载潮与军事合作协议高度相关，暗示部分用户对 OpenAI 商业路线的价值观排斥。若 Claude 维持当前增速，OpenAI 上市估值将面临严重压力。
