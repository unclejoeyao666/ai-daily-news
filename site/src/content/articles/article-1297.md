Google is introducing AI image verification in the Gemini app, allowing users to upload images and ask if they were created or edited using Google AI. The feature uses SynthID, Google's digital watermarking technology that embeds imperceptible signals into AI-generated images. Users can upload any image and ask Gemini directly to check whether it contains SynthID watermarks indicating Google AI origin. Google plans to expand SynthID verification to video and audio, and will support C2PA content credentials in the future as part of broader industry efforts to improve content transparency across the web.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

AI 内容溯源是生成式 AI 规模化的基础设施问题。SynthID 若成为行业标准，将深刻影响新闻摄影、司法取证、网络信息安全等领域。谷歌率先在消费者应用中落地，有望定义 AI 可信度的技术基准。
