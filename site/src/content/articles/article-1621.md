Firestorm Labs has raised $82 million in Series B funding to deploy its xCell containerized drone manufacturing platform to conflict zones. The platform can 3D print complete drone systems in under 24 hours. Drones can be configured for surveillance or electronic warfare, and CEO Dan Magy confirms they are capable of lethal operations. The company began as a drone maker but pivoted when customers requested moving production closer to the front lines. Investors include Washington Harbour Partners, NEA, Ondas, In-Q-Tel, Lockheed Martin, Booz Allen Ventures, and others. Total funding now stands at $153 million. The Pentagon has made contested logistics one of six national crisis priorities.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

集装箱工厂开到战区，标志着战场后勤正在被 AI 驱动的分布式制造重新定义。Firestorm 能 24 小时出无人机，且配置灵活，是"战争民主化"的典型案例。军事 AI 正在从后台支持走向前线生产，这将深刻改变未来战争的经济学。
