Lovable has launched its vibe-coding mobile app on iOS and Android, allowing developers to build web apps using voice or text AI prompts from anywhere. The app works autonomously after receiving user input and syncs progress between phone and computer, with notifications when builds are ready. The launch comes shortly after Apple's App Store crackdown on vibe-coding tools, with Apple blocking updates to Replit and Vibecode for downloading new code or changing functionality. Apple clarified it was not banning vibe-coding apps themselves, but preventing apps that dynamically modify their functionality post-approval, which presents security risks and prevents proper App Review vetting.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

苹果的监管收紧让 vibe-coding 赛道重新回到"功能边界"的讨论。Lovable 逆势上线说明市场需求的真实性已足以支撑开发者绕过平台限制。移动端 vibe-coding 若能站稳脚跟，将把 AI 编程从小众极客习惯扩展为真正的移动生产力场景。
