Amazon has launched a new 'Join the chat' feature on product pages that allows users to ask questions about products and receive conversational audio responses generated in real time. The AI pulls together insights about product features, customer feedback, and other relevant information to provide natural, discussion-style answers. The feature builds on previous responses to provide more relevant information without repetition, aiming to replicate the experience of speaking with a knowledgeable store employee. It is part of the broader 'Hear the highlights' experience offering short audio summaries on millions of product pages within the Amazon Shopping app.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

亚马逊将 AI 音频对话深度嵌入购物决策链，从"搜索商品"升级为"与商品对话"。这对转化率和用户粘性有直接影响，也可能重塑电商业的客服标准。语音交互正在成为电商体验的新入口。
