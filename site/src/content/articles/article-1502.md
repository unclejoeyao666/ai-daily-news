OpenAI responded to concerns about its sales growth, stating that its consumer and enterprise businesses are 'firing on all cylinders' and that it continues to see growth in demand from business customers and its nascent advertising business. The company declared the internal mood 'incredibly positive.' The Wall Street Journal reported that OpenAI fell short of several internal targets as rivals gained ground, with CFO Sarah Friar and other leaders reportedly concerned that the company might not be able to pay for future computing contracts if revenue doesn't grow fast enough. OpenAI described the WSJ report as 'prime clickbait.' Glasswing Ventures Founder & Managing Partner Rudina Seseri commented on Bloomberg Businessweek Daily.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

OpenAI 的公关反击与其 IPO 时间线高度同步。内部目标Miss 加上卸载率飙升，揭示了商业化压力与增长放缓并存的结构性矛盾。若收入增速持续落后于算力成本，IPO 故事线将面临资本市场重新定价。
