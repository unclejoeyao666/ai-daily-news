SoftBank is creating a new company called Roze AI that aims to automate data center construction in the U.S. using autonomous robots to deploy and build server farms. The company is being prepped for an IPO, with some executives pushing for a listing in the second half of 2026 at a desired valuation of $100 billion, according to the Financial Times and Wall Street Journal. SoftBank has previously backed ambitious but failed ventures like Zume Pizza. Some within SoftBank have expressed skepticism about the valuation and IPO timeline. This venture aligns with a broader trend of using AI and automation to modernize industrial sectors, as seen in Jeff Bezos' Project Prometheus.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

千亿美元 IPO 目标折射出软银对 AI 驱动工业自动化的押注狂想。数据中心是 AI 军备竞赛的基础设施，用机器人建数据中心——逻辑自洽，但工程复杂度远超消费级应用。其 IPO 时序暗示软银正将叙事从"科技投资"升级为"AI 工业化"。
