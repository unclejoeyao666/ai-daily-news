Google is launching Gemini 3, its most intelligent model that helps bring any idea to life. Gemini 3 Pro delivers unparalleled results across every major AI benchmark compared to previous versions and surpasses 2.5 Pro at coding, mastering both agentic workflows and complex zero-shot tasks. It integrates into existing production agent and coding workflows while enabling new use cases in multimodal understanding, visual reasoning, and spatial reasoning. Developers can access Gemini 3 Pro through the Gemini API in Google AI Studio and Vertex AI, or use the Google Antigravity platform for agentic development. The model also unlocks 'vibe coding' in Google AI Studio, letting users create apps from natural language prompts.

## 对 AI 行业的影响

Gemini 3 的发布标志着多模态 AI 竞争进入新阶段。编程能力再进化意味着 AI 辅助开发的可靠性进一步提升，智能体工作流的产业落地有望加速。
